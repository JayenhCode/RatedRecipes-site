Prepare yourselves!

These are the best brownie cookies!

We made these last weekend and we cannot stop eating them.

Now it is time to share the recipe!

    Ingredients needed:

  • 1 cup water
  • 1/2 cup flour
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup chocolate chips
  • 1 cup cocoa powder

        • Put 1 cup water into a bowl
        Put 1 cup cocoa powder into a bowl
        Put 1/2 cup flour into the bowl with water
        *stir the bowl of water & flour*
        Put 1 cup sugar into a different bowl
        2 eggs into the bowl with sugar
        *stir sugar and eggs*
        *mix both bowls together and stir*
        Add 1 cup of chocolate chips into mix and stir
        *put into Brownie glass tin*

        put brownie cookies into oven 400 degrees 20 minutes

        enjoy
