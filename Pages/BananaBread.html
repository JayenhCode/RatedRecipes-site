Prepare yourselves!

This is some great banana bread.

We made some this weekend and it was gone in a day!

Now it is time to share the recipe!

    Ingredients needed:

  • 1 cup water

  • 1/2 cup flour

  • 1 cup sugar

  • 2 eggs

  • 1 banana

        • Put 1 cup water into a bowl

        Put 1/2 cup flour into the bowl with water

        *stir the bowl of water & flour*

        Put 1 cup sugar into a different bowl

        2 eggs into the bowl with sugar

        *stir sugar and eggs*

        *mix both bowls together and stir*

        Add 1 chopped banana into mix and stir

        *pour into bread tin*

        put tin into oven 400 degrees 20 minutes

        enjoy
